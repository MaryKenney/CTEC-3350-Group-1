4. Audience

  1. Who’s our target audience?

    • Anyone who enjoys a good live performance and theatre arts

  2. What do they think of us?

    • They’re fun and offer good service and entertainment for the price. It’s enjoyable to come and eat there and watch the shows.

  3. Why should they care?

    • Pocket Sandwich Theatre is a family friendly one of a kind attraction, classical in the very verbiage of the word theatre. In the current monotonous state of the world during the tragedy that is COVID-19 we believe that Pocket Sandwich Theatre can provide a “new” but familiar way to experience both tragedy and comedy through the visceral escapism that is live theatre. Embrace tradition, reject modernality.

  4. Be specific, provide information such as age, gender, race, income level, education level, etc.

    • Very open and can be appealing to people of all backgrounds regardless of age, gender, race and education level.
    • Because dinner services are not included in the ticket price, income level may play a factor in who comes in. Ticket prices are more expensive than other entertainment options, such as regular cinema which may be more appealing to people on tighter budgets. As a result, those with a lower income may fall out of the range of the target audience.

    • Large- Corporate movie theatres or places with large-scale theatre productions (such as Bass Hall). Fast food or corporate restaurants.

  2. What are they telling the audience that WE should also be telling them?

    • Their tagline is “The most fun you can have in a Dallas Theatre,” which I feel is something that is important to emphasize in their website as well. This is a place where you can have a fun night, eat good food, and interact and grow in the DFW community.

  3. What makes us different and unique compared to our competitors?

    • We’re interactive, fun and casual, we have award-winning shows and actors, and we pride ourselves in supporting local actors and playwrights.
